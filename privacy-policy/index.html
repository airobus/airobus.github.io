Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to GamePoke ("we", "our", "this site"). GamePoke is a free online gaming platform that allows you to play games directly in your browser. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Basic Server Logs: Standard web server logs (IP address, user agent, referrer) for security and troubleshooting. This data is used in aggregate to understand site reliability.

  • Analytics (if enabled): We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand anonymized traffic trends (page views, referrers). We do not build personal profiles.

    • Local Storage

    Some features may store small values in your browser via localStorage:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)

  • Language preference

    • These values stay on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. You can clear them anytime via your browser settings.

    Third-Party Games

    Games on GamePoke are embedded from third-party sources. Each game may have its own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of individual game providers.

    External Links

    We may link to third-party sites. These sites have their own privacy policies. Please review them when leaving our site.

    Children's Privacy

    Some games may not be suitable for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can delete it.

    Data Security

    We follow industry-standard practices to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "lastUpdated" date above.
